ABOUT US

Terri Z. Campbell, CFA Founder, Managing Principal
Before starting Archer Bay Capital, Terri spent twenty-five years managing money for large institutions – banks, insurance, pensions, endowments, and mutual funds. She knew the investment process used by these firms worked for individual investors as well, but too often she saw individual portfolios that were overly complex, with sub-par investment returns and a lack of transparency. Terri started Archer Bay Capital to offer the personal financial advice and solid investment performance that she saw was missing - in a cost-effective way.
Terri earned an MBA from Cornell University, a BA from The Ohio State University, and is a Chartered Financial Analyst.
Community involvement is important to Terri and she has served on the Board of the Big Sister Association of Greater Boston for more than 10 years in addition to supporting numerous charities focused on social services, children, and animal welfare.
Kerstin E. Ramstrom, CFA, Managing Director
Prior to joining Archer Bay Capital in 2022, Kerstin spent more than fifteen years as a professional investor at mutual fund companies, banks, and insurance companies. She has always been passionate about teaching others about how investing can support their long-term life goals.
Kerstin received an MBA from Cornell University and holds a BA in Math and Physics from Skidmore College. She is also a Chartered Financial Analyst.
In her free time, Kerstin coaches MBA students interested in careers in the investment business and is also passionate about SCUBA diving and holistic health.
